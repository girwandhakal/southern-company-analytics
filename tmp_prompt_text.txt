End‑of‑Sale (
EoS
) 
and

EoL

As a result, we struggle to:
Proactively identify aging or unsupported equipment
Align refresh timing with risk, cost, and operational impact
Ensure optimal SmartNet coverage and compliance
Provide clear, executive‑level insight into lifecycle risk
Cisco defines clear lifecycle milestones (
EoS
, 
EoL
